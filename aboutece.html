 ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

About

                             

Electronics and Communications Engineering (ECE) is the application of science and mathematics to practical problems in the field of electronics and communications. Electronics and communications engineers engage in research, design, development and testing of the electronic equipment used in various systems. This field is closely aligned with Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and the advances in CSE play a significant role in the advancement of ECE. This is why a degree in ECE is always in high demand.This branch primarily deals with areas such as:Analog transmissionBasic electronicsSolid state devicesMicroprocessors and microcontrollersDigital and analog communicationAnalog integrated circuitsSatellite communicationMicrowave engineeringAntennae and wave progression.It also deals with the manufacturing of electronic devices, circuits, and communications equipment.BML Munjal University equips students with all-round skills to carve a rewarding career for themselves.Career ProspectBMU trains students to design, develop, test, and supervise the manufacturing of electrical equipment. The candidates are provided comprehensive knowledge of electrical equipment such as navigation systems, radar, electric motors, communication systems, and more. The following areas will require highly qualified engineers:A CSE graduate with a good academic record can work in different fields such as:MicroelectronicsVLSIEmbedded SystemsSatellite & Wireless CommunicationsRF & Microwave DesignRobotics & MechatronicsInternet of Things
